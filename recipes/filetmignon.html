Filet Mignon

This is a chocolate chip cookie wow

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients
  2. Mix wet ingerdients
  3. Combine both dry and wet into singular bowl
  4. add chocolate chips
  5. put dough on baking sheet
  6. bake for 30 mins on 375 degrees farenheit
  7. let cool for 10 mins
